Stock Alerts Pro

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Stock Alerts Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Natts Digital. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service at stockalertspro.nattsdigital.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Username, email address, and name when you register
  • Authentication data: Encrypted password hash (we never store plain text passwords)
  • Pi Network data: Pi UID and Pi username when you authenticate via Pi Network
  • Alert preferences: Stock symbols, price targets, and alert types you configure
  • Notification settings: Telegram Chat ID if you enable Telegram notifications
  • Payment data: Stripe customer ID and subscription status (no card details stored — handled by Stripe)
  • Usage data: Login timestamps, last login date

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the Stock Alerts Pro service:

  • To send you stock price alert notifications via Telegram
  • To manage your subscription and billing
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • To maintain your alert preferences and settings
  • To communicate service updates or important account information

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

ℹ️ Pi Network users: When accessing Stock Alerts Pro via Pi Browser, only Pi Network authentication and Pi payments are available. Stripe payments are not presented to Pi Browser users.

  • Stripe — Payment processing for premium subscriptions. Stripe handles all card data securely. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Pi Network — Optional authentication and payment via Pi Browser. See Pi Network's Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram — Delivery of stock alert notifications. Your Telegram Chat ID is stored only to send you alerts you have configured.
  • Yahoo Finance (yfinance) — Real-time stock price data. No personal data is shared with Yahoo Finance.
  • Supabase — Database hosting for your account and alert data.
  • Render — Application hosting infrastructure.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase's hosted PostgreSQL database. We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Session tokens are randomly generated and rotated on each login
  • Single-session enforcement prevents simultaneous logins

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide our service.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@nattsdigital.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for Telegram notifications at any time via Settings

8. Children's Privacy

Stock Alerts Pro is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
